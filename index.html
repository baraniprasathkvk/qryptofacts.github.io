Intro

Bitcoin(BTC) is a decentralized digital currency,
enables instant payments to anyone in the world. It 
is a peer-to-peer technology.

Laszlo Hanyecz made the first transaction by 
buying pizzas celebrated as ‘Bitcoin Pizza Day’.
