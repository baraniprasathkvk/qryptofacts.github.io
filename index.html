Intro

Bitcoin Gold(BTG)  is a distributed digital 
currency. It is a hard fork of Bitcoin and 
Opensource. BTG aims to fix  monopolization of 
mining and used to solve blocks

2

Features

BTG was created to help address the problem of 
centralization in Bitcoin. Bitcoin network received 
BTG on a ratio of 1BTC:1BTG.
It uses SIGHASH_FORK_ID replay protection.

Founder
