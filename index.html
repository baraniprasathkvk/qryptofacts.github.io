1

Intro

BitShares is based on Graphene, an open source 
C++ blockchain mechanism. It launched under 
ProtoShares(PTS); later renamed to BitShares.

2

About

BitShares is an industrial grade “crypto-equity”, 
peer to peer distributed ledger and network based 
on a Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS) algorithm. 
Crypto-based assets track like the US dollar 
denominated “BitAsset” known as bitUSD.

3

Features

BitShares system block time is 3 seconds.
The network has a maximum BTS coins supply 
is 3.6 billion.

4

Price

 A SmartCoin is a cryptocurrency whose value is 
pegged to that of another asset, such as US Dollar 
or gold. SmartCoins provide the freedom of 
cryptocurrency with the stability of the dollar.
